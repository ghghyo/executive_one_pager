CHALLENGE

Manual claims verification and denials cost U.S. providers $262B annually, driven by complex workflows and labor constraints.

SOLUTION

We’re building automation that observes how staff work and quietly creates workflows in the background. It tracks repetitive admin tasks across EMRs and payer portals—recognizing patterns and executing them automatically. From eligibility checks to claim follow-ups, TNT turns manual clicks and calls into continuous revenue recovery.

MARKET
