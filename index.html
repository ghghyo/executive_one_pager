CHALLENGE

The healthcare industry faces a massive $262 billion annual crisis from claim denials and delayed payments, a problem driven by complex, manual administrative processes and a shrinking workforce. These inefficiencies result in lost revenue, administrative burden, and poor patient experiences.

SOLUTION

We are building AI-powered voice agents that act as supercharged virtual staff. Our agents handle up to 3x the call volume of human teams, integrate directly with EMR and payer systems, and reduce administrative costs by tens of thousands of dollars monthly. We help providers recover revenue, faster, and at lower cost.
